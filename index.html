Who am I?

I'm a proud veteran, brother, dog dad, and leader. I come from an IT background, where I cut my teeth on a system and network administrator role in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom and Operation New Dawn. Coming home, I found myself working on a number of project-based contracts with partners and clients that include the U.S. Army, Air Force, Secret Service, and the state of Massachusetts.

After nearly a decade working with web-based applications on the hardware and networking side of tech, I realized that I wanted more than just the taste of programming that I got through testing and configuration. This led me to pursue a career in development, and to your screen! Some of the development skills, techniques and tools I've learned so far include:
